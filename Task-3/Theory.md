### **Question 1: What new improvements were introduced in Gemini 3.0?**

**Answer:**
Gemini 3.0 introduced **Deep Think** for stronger problem solving, a more **interactive and creative UI**, better support for **agent based coding**, and much better understanding of **text, images, and videos**.

---

### **Question 2: How does Gemini 3.0 improve coding and automation workflows?**

**Answer:**
Gemini 3.0 can now **plan, write, run, and debug code automatically**.
It could do this before too, but now it is **faster, clearer, and more powerful**.
With **Vibe Coding**, you can give simple instructions, and it will create smart logic using Deep Think.
It can also handle **much larger projects** because of its **1 million token context**.

---

### **Question 3: How does Gemini 3.0 improve multimodal understanding?**

**Answer:**
Gemini 3.0 understands **text, images, videos, audio, and documents** together with higher accuracy.
It can reason better with videos, read **PDFs, charts, and screenshots** more clearly, and it can even improve **image and video resolution**.

---
